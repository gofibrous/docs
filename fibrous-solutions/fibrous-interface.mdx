The Fibrous interface has been redesigned with a modular structure to deliver complex DeFi routings with the simplicity of one-click operations and to diversify your trading options with various features. **Swap**, **Batch Swap**, and **Limit Order** modules are presented in a way that allows you to easily explore and utilize them.

Additionally, to make your optimized trading experience more comprehensible and to ensure that full control remains with you, the interface features a clear, simple, and intuitive design. This user-centric approach ensures that even as you execute complex trading strategies, the process remains straightforward and transparent.
