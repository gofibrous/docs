---
title: "Fibrous Interface"
description: "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication"
---

Fibrous interface features a modular design that simplifies complex DeFi routing into single-click operations and diversifies trading options for users. Core features like **Swap, Batch Swap,** and **Limit Order** are presented as distinct modules, making them easy to explore and utilize.

To ensure the optimized trading experience is both comprehensible and gives users full control, the interface is built on a clean, simple, and intuitive design. This user-centric approach guarantees that even when executing sophisticated trading strategies, the process remains straightforward and transparent.
